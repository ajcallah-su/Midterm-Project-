Braun Bay

Located in Moultonboro, a 30 minute boat ride from Wolfeoboro towndocks is Braun Bay, a popular sand bar on Lake Winnipeasukee. Braun Bay is the place to be on the Fourth of July, however, it remains a popular location any day of the summer.

Mount Major

Located in Alton, just 7 miles south of Ellacoya State Park, a short hike up Mt. Major (1,785 feet) provides spectacular panoramic views of Lake Winnipesaukee.

The Mount Washington

The M/S Mount Washington visits five different ports on alternating days throughout the week. Weirs Beach is the Mount Washington’s home port and offers cruises every day. All cruises are 2½ hours roundtrip.
